The steps for prepare dish

Curd:
Use fresh curd that is not sour testing. You can use homemade cord nor greek yogurt then you can skip straining and use it straight away.
Flavoring:
I have used cardamom powder and saffron for flavouring.
Sweetener:
Powder sugar is add to sweeten the dessert. You can adjust the amount of sugar to suit your taste.
Whisking:
After adding sugar whisk the yogurt very well using a hand whisk until smooth. If you are doing in large quantities then you can also use a hand blender or a food processor.
